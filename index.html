How to play

A zombie carrying a Chinese word (with pinyin) walks toward the bear.

⚔️ Tap the correct meaning to shoot it down. The farther away it still is, the bigger the bonus — and streaks multiply your score.

☝️ You get one shot per word. A wrong tap makes the zombie charge and take a heart — no second guesses. The correct answer flashes green so you learn it for next time.

🐌 Too slow counts too: if the zombie reaches the bear, that's a heart gone. Three hearts and the round is over.

🔊 Every word shows pinyin and can be heard aloud — on zombies, in flashcards, and in the missed-words review.

🃏 Learn mode drills the same word pool as flashcards first, so you can study, then fight.
