How to play

A lucky cat 🐱 strolls in from the right carrying a Chinese word (with pinyin) on a flag.

🧧 Tap the correct meaning and the cat cheers, flips a gold coin 🪙, and you score! The farther away the cat still is, the bigger the bonus — and streaks multiply your score.

☝️ You get one shot per word. A wrong tap costs a heart ❤️ — no second guesses. The correct answer flashes green so you learn it for next time.

🐌 Too slow counts too: if the cat wanders all the way across without an answer, that costs a heart. Three hearts and the round is over.

🔊 Every word shows pinyin and can be heard aloud — during the game, in flashcards, and in the missed-words review.

🃏 Learn mode drills the same word pool as flashcards first, so you can study, then play.
